MORNING ROUTINE

GOOD MORNING
Cleanser

Cleansers are used to remove dirt, oil, makeup, and other impurities from the skin's surface. Cleansing helps prevent breakouts, removes environmental pollutants, and prepares the skin for the absorption of other skincare products.

Toner

Toners help balance the skin's pH levels and remove any remaining traces of dirt or oil after cleansing. Toners can soothe, hydrate, and prepare the skin for better absorption of serums and moisturizers. Some toners also contain ingredients that provide additional benefits, such as antioxidants or exfoliants.

Serum

Serums are concentrated formulations designed to address specific skincare concerns, such as hydration, anti-aging, or brightening. Serums contain potent ingredients that can penetrate the skin deeply, delivering targeted benefits. They are often used to address specific issues beyond what a general moisturizer can provide.

Moisturizer

Moisturizers hydrate the skin, help maintain its natural barrier, and prevent water loss. Even if you have oily skin, moisturizing is crucial. It helps keep the skin balanced, reduces the risk of dryness and irritation, and contributes to a smoother and more supple complexion.

Sunscreen

Sunscreen protects the skin from the harmful effects of UV radiation, including sunburn, premature aging, and an increased risk of skin cancer. Sunscreen is a crucial step in preventing sun damage and maintaining overall skin health. It should be applied daily, even on cloudy days, to protect against both UVA and UVB rays.
