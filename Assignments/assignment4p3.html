NIGHT ROUTINE

GOOD NIGHT
Cleanser

Cleansers are used to remove dirt, oil, makeup, and other impurities from the skin's surface. Cleansing helps prevent breakouts, removes environmental pollutants, and prepares the skin for the absorption of other skincare products.

Retinol

Retinol accelerates skin cell turnover, promoting the shedding of old, damaged skin cells and the generation of new ones. This can lead to smoother and more even-toned skin. Retinol is often used as a nighttime treatment because it can make the skin more sensitive to sunlight. It's crucial to use sunscreen during the day when using retinol.

Eye Cream

Eye creams are formulated to provide intense hydration to this sensitive area, helping to combat dryness and reduce the appearance of fine lines caused by dehydration.

Moisturizer

Moisturizers hydrate the skin, help maintain its natural barrier, and prevent water loss. Even if you have oily skin, moisturizing is crucial. It helps keep the skin balanced, reduces the risk of dryness and irritation, and contributes to a smoother and more supple complexion.
